Welcome to IPTV Casper Setup
============================

This wizard will guide you through the installation of IPTV Casper, a modern IPTV player with advanced features.

IPTV Casper Features:
---------------------
  * Watch Live TV, VOD, and TV Series
  * M3U Playlist and Xtream Codes support
  * Floating Window (Picture-in-Picture) mode
  * Always-on-top functionality for multitasking
  * Modern, user-friendly interface
  * Hardware-accelerated video playback
  * Custom branding with professional design

System Requirements:
--------------------
  * Windows 10 (version 1809 or later) or Windows 11
  * 64-bit operating system
  * 4GB RAM minimum (8GB recommended)
  * Internet connection for streaming
  * Modern graphics card with hardware acceleration

Installation Size:
------------------
  * Approximately 80 MB of disk space required

Before You Begin:
-----------------
  * Close any running IPTV applications
  * Ensure you have administrator privileges
  * Have your IPTV playlist or Xtream Codes credentials ready

Important Notes:
----------------
  * IPTV Casper is a media player only
  * Users are responsible for their content sources
  * The application does not provide, host, or distribute IPTV content
  * Ensure you have the right to access any content you stream
