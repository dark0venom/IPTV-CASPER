IPTV Casper Installation Complete!
===================================

Thank you for installing IPTV Casper v1.0.0

What's Next?
------------

1. Launch IPTV Casper:
   - Click Finish to launch the application now
   - Or find it in your Start Menu

2. Getting Started:
   - Add your M3U playlist or Xtream Codes credentials
   - Browse channels and start watching
   - Try the Floating Window feature for multitasking

3. Floating Window (Picture-in-Picture):
   - Play any channel or video
   - Click the PiP button (top-right corner)
   - Use the pin button to keep window always on top
   - Continue working while watching!

Quick Tips:
-----------
  * Desktop Shortcut: Created (if selected)
  * Start Menu: Find under "IPTV Casper"
  * Keyboard Shortcuts: Space (play/pause), F (fullscreen), M (mute)
  * Floating Window: Perfect for watching while working
