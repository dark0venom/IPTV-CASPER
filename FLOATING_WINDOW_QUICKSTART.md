- Click and drag the bottom-right corner resize handle
- Window automatically maintains 16:9 aspect ratio

**Toggle Always-on-Top:**
- Click the **pin button** (ğŸ“Œ) in the title bar

- Click the X button in the title bar
- Or click the Picture-in-Picture button again in the main window

### Window doesn't appear?
- Make sure you selected a channel first
- Check if the channel is working in the main window
